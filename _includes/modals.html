Modals

A Default Modal with a .wide form inside. Besides this, if you add .center to a modal it will be truly centered on Desktop size.

Modals are stackable (try attach-image) and may also replace an existing one (try ? next to new topic).
All Modals will be appended to a dedicated Container Element .modal-container.
